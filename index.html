Turning odd jobs into opportunities

Errandz is a mobile platform software application that serves as a solution for people who are looking for odd jobs to earn quick money.

From high school kids who are too young to be working full time to retired handy or helpful hands, your neighbourhood can get help with everyday chores like babysitting, lawn mowing etc. will find this application extremely useful.

Features

Errandz is a mobile application platform with a dashboard both for the hirer and the service provider(tasker). Tasker can find nearby jobs along with the respective descriptions, all listed according to the skills/preferences provided by him. An edit profile option is provided, complete with details, reviews and ratings for the taskers to look at before sending a request.The user can mark the job as done, once he is finished with it and has been paid for it. Hirers can find taskers conveniently by adding a job that they want to be done, complete with job description and all the details.
