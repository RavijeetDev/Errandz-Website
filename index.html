Turning odd jobs into opportunities

Errandz is a mobile platform software application that serves as a solution for people who are looking for odd jobs to earn quick money.

From high school kids who are too young to be working full time to retired handy or helpful hands, your neighbourhood can get help with everyday chores like babysitting, lawn mowing etc. will find this application extremely useful.

Features

Errandz is a mobile application platform with a dashboard both for the hirer and the service provider(tasker). Tasker can find nearby jobs along with the respective descriptions, all listed according to the skills/preferences provided by him. An edit profile option is provided, complete with details, reviews and ratings for the taskers to look at before sending a request.The user can mark the job as done, once he is finished with it and has been paid for it. Hirers can find taskers conveniently by adding a job that they want to be done, complete with job description and all the details. It’s a reliable and secure platform for searching jobs and hiring taskers. Security is ensured by revealing the address only when the job has been assigned to the tasker.

FAQ's

1. What is errandz and How can I post jobs on Errandz?

It is a mobile platform software application that serves as a solution for people who are looking for odd jobs to earn quick money. Log into your errandz account , choose post a job and add the description of the job with wage, location, date, time, job category and all other necessary details you want to provide to the worker.

2. How do I hire somebody to get the job done?

After you have posted a job you will get requests on who is interested in doing the job. You can cancel or approve the request of the worker depending on if they are a good fit for your job.

3. Will the jobs that I posted be visible at the top of the list to the person who is finding a job?

Yes, all the jobs that you have posted will be visible to the worker in “Near by” jobs category.

4. How much will it cost to hire somebody to get the job done?

It is not going to cost you anything for the hiring process. All that will cost is how much you are paying the worker. Workers will mostly be based on hourly rates that you can negotiate once you hire the worker.

5. Can I know about the work performance of the person I am hiring before hiring?

Yes, you certainly can review the profile of the worker you are hiring before hiring based on the feedback provided by previous clients he worked with and you can also leave your ratings on the workers profile based on how well he worked with you.

6. How will Errandz help anyone who needs a job?

Errandz provided you odd jobs that can be easily done and are available in nearby locations. So if you are looking for quick way to earn money with odd jobs this is the best platform for you.

7. How will I get alerts for jobs?

In the section “upcoming jobs” you will see the list of jobs client has posted. You can accept the job if you think you are the best fit for it or you can simply decline the request. You can always save the job for later but it won’t guarantee that someone else did not already took the job.

8. What’s required to start getting job alerts on errandz?

You should have an account on Errandz with all the personal information verified that will be securely stored with our privacy policy. You should be able to travel to at least nearby locations and have a smartphone to get alerts from the clients and manage the jobs.

9. What types of jobs can I do as someone who needs job quickly?

You can do all sorts of odd jobs like pet sitting, cleaning, organizing, packing, unpacking, mounting, moving, home repairs, painting, carpentry or yard work etc.

10. How much will I get paid?

All such information will be mentioned on the “job description” column by the client who is posting the job. Wages will mainly be based on hourly rates which is best for most independent workers.
