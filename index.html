Turning odd jobs into opportunities

Errandz is a mobile platform software application that serves as a solution for people who are looking for odd jobs to earn quick money. This application will provide them with an opportunity to earn some pocket money by working for a couple of hours and learn some skills along the way.

Features

Our mobile application provides a dashboard for both the hirer and tasker(service provider). The tasker will be able to see the available jobs on the job list along with the respective descriptions, all listed according to the skills/preferences provided by him. An edit profile option is provided, complete with details, reviews and ratings for the taskers to look at before sending a request. The user can mark the job as done, once he is finished with it and has been paid for it. Utmost care will be given to the safety and security of the users.

Find Nearby Odd Jobs

The tasker will be able to see the available jobs on the job list.

Reliable and Secure Platform

The option to rate and review(works both for hirer and tasker), and to give feedback make it a reliable application

Find Taskers Conveniently

The hirer will be able to look for taskers within 10km radius of current location
